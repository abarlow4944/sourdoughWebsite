Sourdough Discard Blondies

This is one of the easiest ways to use up your sourdough discard. Perfectly chewy and caramel-y with a nutty aroma, you can't go wrong with these blondies!

Sourdough Discard Blondies

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 25 minutes
  • Yield: 16-20 brownies

Ingredients

  • 240g all purpose flour
  • 100g sourdough discard
  • 4 tsp (20g) vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 400g light brown sugar
  • 226g melter butter
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Sliced almonds (optional)
  • Roasted walnuts (optional)

Instructions

1. Preheat the Oven
Preheat the oven to 176Â°C and line a 9" x 9" metal baking dish with parchment paper.

2. Mix the Wet Ingredients
Melt the butter in the microwave or on the stovetop. Mix the butter, the sourdough discard, and egg to a large bowl and whisk until smooth.

3. Mix the Dry Ingredients
In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.

4. Make the Batter
Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, as well as the roasted walnuts (amount to your liking) and stir with a spatula to combine (do not overmix!). Pour the batter into the baking dish and sprinkle the top with sliced almonds.

6. Bake
Bake for 40-50 minutes. Let the blondies cool completely until cutting into squares. Enjoy!

Tips & Tricks

  • If you prefer your blondies less sweet, decrease the amount of sugar in the recipe by 50-100g.
  • To check if your brownies are done baking in the oven, do the toothpick test: poke a toothpick into the center of the cake and pull it out. If the toothpick comes out cleanly, it's done! If not, it needs more time.
