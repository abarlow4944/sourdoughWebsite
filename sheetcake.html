Sourdough Discard Sheet Cake

A classic vanilla sheet cake with cream cheese frosting that is guaranteed to be a hit. This recipe is perfect especially when you have a lot of sourdough discard to use up.

Sourdough Discard SheetCake

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 30 minutes
  • Yield: 20-25 slices

Ingredients

    Cake:
  • 250g all-purpose flour
  • 190g melted butter
  • 125g whole milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 200g sourdough discard
  • 9g baking powder
  • 5g baking soda
  • 250g granulated sugar
  • 4g salt
  • 2 tsp (10g) vanilla extract

    • Cream Cheese Frosting:
  • 250g cream cheese
  • 50g butter
  • 300g powdered sugar
  • 5g vanilla extract
  • 2g salt

Instructions

1. Preheat the Oven
Preheat the oven to 180Â°C and prepare a 9x13 baking dish with parchment paper.

2. Mix the Wet Ingredients
In a small bowl combine the melted butter, eggs, vanilla, milk, and sourdough starter.

3. Mix the Wet Ingredients
In a large bowl, combine the floure, baking powder, baking soda, sugar, and salt.

4. Make the Batter
Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix with a spatula until just combined (don't overmix!).

5. Bake
Pour the batter into the baking dish and bake for 30 minutes or until the cake is golden brown.

6. Make the Frosting
Add all of the ingredients into a bowl and beat on speed 8 of your stand mixer until light and fluffy (2 minutes).

7. Frost the Cake
Once the cake has completely cooled, spread the frosting onto the cake and top with sprinkles. Allow to sit for 15 minutes before slicing. Enjoy!

Tips & Tricks

  • If you have leftover frosting, you can freeze it to use later as a spread for sourdough discard cinnamon rolls!
  • To check if your cake is done baking in the oven, do the toothpick test: poke a toothpick into the center of the cake and pull it out. If the toothpick comes out cleanly, it's done! If not, it needs more time.
