Sourdough Discard Cheez-Its

These sourdough discard crackers taste exactly like store-bought Cheez-Its (no really, you'll be shocked)! The only difference is that the homemade version is free of all the preservatives and chemicals found in the boxed crackers.

Sourdough Discard Cheez-Its

  • Prep time: 30 minutes
  • Cook time: 15 minutes
  • Yield: 100+ crackers

Ingredients

  • 226g cheddar cheese, chopped
  • 85g sourdough discard
  • 28g unsalted butter, chopped
  • 5g ranch seasoning
  • 65g all-purpose flour
  • flaky sea salt

Instructions

1. Blend the Ingredients
Blend all ingredients (except for the flaky sea salt) in a food processor.

2. Chill the Dough
Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and place in the fridge for at least 20 minutes.

3. Preheat the Oven
Preheat the oven to 180Â°C and prepare two baking sheets with parchment paper.

4. Roll Out the Dough
Remove the dough from the fridge and split it in half. Roll out half the dough on the parchment paper as thinly as possible. Cut the crackers into small squares using a pizza cutter, and poke a hole in the center of each cracker with a toothpick. Sprinkle the tops with flaky sea salt. Repeat with the other portion of dough. Don't separate the crackers, they'll separate as they shrink in the oven.

5. Bake
Bake the crackers for 18 minutes, or until a deep golden orange colour. After the crackers bake, leave them in the oven with the door open to let them dry out further. Let the crackers cool before serving. Enjoy!

Tips & Tricks

  • Roll out the dough as thinly as possible - the thinner the crispier!
  • Store the crackers in an air-tight container to prevent them from drying out.
  • If you don't have ranch seasoning, sprinkle in some dill, garlic powder, onion powder, and parsley instead.
