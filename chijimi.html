Sourdough Discard Chijimi

These veggie pancakes are ready to eat in just 10 minutes. So simple, yet so delicious, this is the perfect recipe for you when you need both a quick lunch and a quick way to use up your sourdough discard.

Sourdough Discard Chijimi

  • Prep time: 5 hours
  • Cook time: 5 minutes
  • Yield: 1 large pancake

Ingredients

  • Flour (doesn't matter what kind!)
  • Sourdough discard
  • Water
  • 1 egg
  • Half a packet of Japanese dashi seasoning
  • Toppings of choice, sliced (cabbage, green onion, carrot etc)
  • Soy sauce
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sesame Oil
  • Sesame Seeds

Instructions

1. Preheat the Skillet
Oil a skillet and preheat it at medium-high.

2. Mix the Ingredients
Mix the egg and sourdough discard in a bowl. Add half a packet of Japanese dashi seasoning and mix some more. Add some flour (roughly 1/2 cup) and add water to adjust until you reach a thick pancake-like consistency. Add your vegetables of choice and mix until the toppings are coated in the batter.

3. Cook the Pancake.
Once the skillet has visible steam coming off of it, pour the batter to create one large pancake. Cook on each side for roughly 2-3 minutes, or until golden brown.

4. Cut the Pancake
Cut the pancake into smaller pieces and serve with okonomiyaki sauce. Alternatively, you can make a homemade sauce by mixing equal parts soy sauce and rice vinegar with a small amount of sesame oil (adjust measurements to your liking). Sprinkle in some sesame seeds into the sauce. Enjoy!

Tips & Tricks

  • This is a very laid-back recipe that follows no strict measurements (go by vibes!). Feel free to adjust until you reach your desired taste.
